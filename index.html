How the YST works

Fill the screener

Access the tool from your phone, tablet, or computer. Then, answer 32 questions using your current knowledge of the youth and your clinical judgement.

Receive risk assessment scores

Evaluates warning signs that indicate your youth is more susceptible to being lured and/or recruited and immediate warning signs that indicate the possibility of active involvement.

Document and reassess over time

Record results clearly in the case file with attention to confidentiality. Re-screen during transitions or after drastic changes.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about features, privacy, and empirical validation.

The York-Simcoe Sex Trafficking Screening Tool (YST) screens for sex trafficking recruitment and exploitation risk. Using which, it provides immediate results to support safety and next steps. The tool informs but does not replace judgement by AHT professionals.
The YST screens for sex trafficking risk across two subscales. Recruitment warning signs indicate your youth is more susceptible to being lured and/or recruited. Exploitation warning signs indicate the possibility of active involvement.
High risk groups can include girls and young women, middle adolescents aged 14-17, youth with developmental disabilities, youth with mental health challenges, and LGBTQIA+ youth. Learn more here.
Yes. Specifically, youth with intersectional identities are vulnerable to sexual exploitation. Indigenous youth and Black youth are especially at risk. Learn more here.
Yes! The YST was developed through a rigorous process combining research evidence and the expertise of child welfare workers. It was then tested with over 600 youth across two Ontario child welfare agencies, at two different time points. This process resulted in a 32-item screener that produces both exploitation and recruitment risk scores.
Yes! The YST shows strong reliability, when two different child welfare workers completed it for the same youth, they agreed on scores over 80% of the time.
No data is stored at any point using the YST. All screening is done locally, client-side. You may export a local PDF per your agency's guidelines.
The YST does not require (nor allow) logins to ensure all data is anonymous and untracked.
